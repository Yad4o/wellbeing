The Space

Where the city
meets the sky.

Our 25-metre heated infinity pool sits on the 8th floor, with an unobstructed view of the Bandra skyline. The water temperature is maintained at 29°C year-round. Four poolside cabanas. A dedicated pool bar. Sunrise laps or sunset cocktails — both are equally encouraged.

The pool deck is open to hotel guests, day pass holders, and Wellbeing members. No children under 16. No exceptions.

Pool Rules & Policies

Keep it clean.

We take the pool experience seriously — for everyone's enjoyment, we ask that guests respect the following guidelines.

  • ✦ No glass, ceramic, or breakable items on the pool deck.
  • ✦ No diving or jumping into the pool at any time.
  • ✦ Guests must shower before entering the pool.
  • ✦ No inflatables, pool toys, or water guns.
  • ✦ No photography of other guests without consent.
  • ✦ Minimum age: 16 years. ID may be requested.
  • ✦ Appropriate swimwear only — no shorts, underwear, or street clothes.
  • ✦ Loud music from personal devices is not permitted. The deck has its own curated soundtrack.
  • ✦ Sun beds cannot be reserved. Please do not leave personal items unattended for more than 30 minutes.
  • ✦ Management reserves the right to ask anyone to leave without a refund.
