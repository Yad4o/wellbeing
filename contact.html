The Address

9th Floor, The Loom Building

Bandra West

The building entrance is on Hill Road, between Bandra Gym and the old Carter Road junction. Look for the brass WELLBEING sign at ground level. The reception is on the ground floor — they will direct you to the lift.

No, we don't validate parking. Yes, we recommend not driving.

Getting Here

How to arrive.

🛺

By Auto / Taxi

Bandra (W) station to The Loom Building is approximately 7 minutes by auto. Ask for Hill Road, near Carter Road. Ola and Uber are the most reliable options — drop-off is right at the entrance. Auto from Bandra station: ~₹40–60.

🚇

By Metro

BKC Metro Station (Line 2B) is approximately 12 minutes by auto from the venue. The metro runs until midnight on most days — check the latest schedule. There is no direct metro station in Bandra West yet, but BKC is the closest you'll get.

🚗

By Car / Valet

Valet parking is available from 8PM at ₹200. Pull up to the ground floor entrance and our team will take it from there. Limited parking in the building — we genuinely recommend not driving, especially on weekends. Street parking on Hill Road is unreliable after 7PM.

🚶

On Foot

If you're in Bandra, the walk is pleasant — especially on an evening with good weather. From Bandra station, it's a 15–20 minute walk along Hill Road. You'll pass three good cafes, two bad ones, and one building you've photographed before but don't know why.

Parking Note

We're honest about parking.

It is limited. Bandra on a Saturday night is not the place to look for a parking spot. Valet from 8PM (₹200) is available in the building. Otherwise: Uber, Ola, or the autorickshaw you'll find immediately outside the station. The city has a designated driver problem and we'd rather not be part of it.
