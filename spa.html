About The Ritual

Slow down.
Deliberately.

The Ritual occupies the entire 7th floor. Six treatment rooms, a steam room, a cold plunge, and a relaxation lounge with floor-to-ceiling views. Our therapists are trained in Ayurvedic, Swedish, and deep tissue techniques — blended into treatments you won't find anywhere else in the city.

We use only Subtle Energies and Forest Essentials products. No synthetic fragrances. No shortcuts.

🕯️

Signature Treatments

Our exclusive blends, developed over two years. Indian botanical ingredients, ancient techniques, modern outcomes.

Policies

Good to know.

  • ✦Please arrive 15 minutes before your appointment to change and decompress in the relaxation lounge.
  • ✦Mobile phones are not permitted in the spa beyond the reception area.
  • ✦We do not offer treatments to guests who are intoxicated.
  • ✦Pregnant guests should inform us at booking — some treatments are adjusted or unavailable.
  • ✦Gratuity is at your discretion and entirely separate from the treatment charge.
  • ✦The hydrotherapy circuit (steam + cold plunge) is complimentary with any treatment of 60 min or more.
