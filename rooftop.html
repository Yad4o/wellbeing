The Space

Open air. All year.
Weather permitting.

The rooftop is the newest part of the building — added in the 2021 expansion — and has become the venue's most requested space. It sits on the 9th floor, directly above the main dining room. The view includes the Bandra-Kurla skyline to the north and a sliver of the Arabian Sea to the west on clear evenings.

The retractable roof was installed specifically for Mumbai's monsoon season (June–September). When the rain is heavy, the roof closes automatically and guests move to the main floor with full service continuity. When the rain is light, we leave it open. The petrichor is part of the experience.

Open Air Dinner

Rooftop dinner service runs from 7PM every evening. The full Wellbeing menu is available. Tables are set with linen, candles, and a view that does most of the work for us.

🌙

Friday & Saturday Nights

The rooftop stays open until 4AM on Friday and Saturday. After 10PM it transitions to a cocktail-lounge format. Music stays below 80dB — this is a rooftop, not a rooftop club.

☁️

Monsoon Policy

The retractable roof closes automatically when rainfall exceeds 3mm/hr. Guests are moved to the main floor — same service, same team, different view. We'll top up your drink for the inconvenience.

How to Get Here

Reservations &
Policies

The rooftop operates as a separate reservation from the main dining room. Dinner reservations are available until 9PM. After 9PM, rooftop access requires an existing reservation or member status.

Walk-ins are possible before 9PM subject to availability. After 9PM, the door closes to walk-ins. This is not a policy we apologise for — it's how we keep the space what it is.

  • ✦ Reservations open 30 days in advance for members, 14 days for general guests.
  • ✦ After 9PM: reservation or membership required.
  • ✦ Minimum spend applies after 9PM on Friday & Saturday: ₹2,000 per person.
  • ✦ Weather cancellations: 24-hour notice, full refund. Same-night weather changes: complimentary main floor transfer.
  • ✦ Dress code: smart casual and above. The rooftop has a view — dress for it.
  • ✦ No external food or beverages. No exceptions.
