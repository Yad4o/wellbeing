Why Wellbeing

A venue that works
for you.

A 1940s textile warehouse doesn't become a luxury venue by accident. Every room here has been designed with intention — acoustics, light, sightlines, the way a crowd moves. When you hire it, you get all of that, plus a team that has spent years perfecting events for people who care deeply about them.

We do not do generic. Tell us what you want the evening to feel like — the rest is ours to figure out.

Event Types

We've done all of these.
And done them well.

Corporate

Annual parties, client dinners, off-sites, product reveals. We configure the room to your agenda, not the other way around. AV, branding, and catering packages included.

Birthdays

For the person who doesn't want a "club night" but still wants to dance. Custom menus. A dedicated host. The night starts when you arrive. We handle the rest.

Weddings

Receptions, sangeets, engagement parties. The building is dramatic without trying to be. Intimate enough for 60, large enough for 400. We've cried at a few of these. Good tears.

Product Launches

Fashion, tech, F&B, luxury goods — the venue signals what your brand wants to signal. Media-ready lighting. Flexible configuration. We've seen some excellent launches here.

Screenings

Lounge or Main Floor with projection setup. Film screenings, premieres, documentary debuts. Our sound engineer has done four feature films. It shows in the setup.

Private Concerts

We have a roster of artists who perform privately for the right occasion. Jazz quartets. DJ sets. Classical. Speak to us — if the ask is interesting enough, we'll make it happen.
