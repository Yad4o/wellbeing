Wellbeing  /  FAQ & Policies

The honest answers.

We answer these questions a lot. We try to be direct. If you can't find what you're looking for here, email us.

Dress Code

Smart casual to formal. That means: collared shirts or clean tees, trousers or clean jeans, proper shoes. No athletic wear. No flip flops. No offensive graphics. No visible underwear.

We're not strict. We're consistent.

Age Policy

21 and above after 10PM. No exceptions. Valid government-issued ID required — Aadhaar, passport, or driving licence. We check everyone. Yes, even you.

Before 10PM: 18+. Dining areas are family-friendly until 9PM.

Photography

Personal phones for personal photos — completely fine. Professional cameras, video rigs, or commercial photography requires prior written approval from our team. Don't photograph other guests without asking them first.

We are not Instagram-hostile. We are courtesy-consistent.

Frequently Asked

Things people want to know.

Entry & Access

Is there a cover charge?

Not for dinner reservations. Not for the spa. Not for the pool (day passes are separate). On Friday and Saturday nights after 11PM, a cover charge of ₹500–₹1,000 may apply for non-reservation guests. Members never pay a cover. We tell you this upfront so there are no surprises at the door.

Can I just walk in?

Before 10PM, yes — subject to availability. We always hold a few tables for walk-ins. After 11PM on weekends, walk-ins are at the door team's discretion. They're very reasonable people. But so is making a reservation.

I have a reservation — will I definitely get in?

Yes. A confirmed reservation means a table. We hold it for 20 minutes from your booking time. After that, we may release it. Please call us if you're running late — it's a simple courtesy that helps everyone.

Can I bring a large group?

Groups of 8 or more are considered large and require prior coordination. Email reservations@wellbeing.in at least 72 hours in advance. Groups of 15+ qualify for a semi-private arrangement. Groups of 30+ should look at our Private Events page — that's the right product for you.

Dining

Do you accommodate dietary requirements?

Yes. We're a kitchen that cooks with conviction, but we're also a kitchen that can adapt. Vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, nut-free — please mention this at the time of reservation so Chef Kavya can prepare alternatives. We don't promise everything on the menu translates perfectly, but we promise we'll try hard.

Can I bring a birthday cake from outside?

We'd rather make one for you — and we will, if you give us 24 hours notice. The pastry team takes birthday cake seriously. If you must bring your own, there's a ₹500 cake-cutting fee. This covers the plates, the serving, the candles, and the team's dignity.

Is the menu the same every night?

The core menu is consistent, but Chef Kavya changes specials weekly based on what's at market. If something you loved isn't on the current menu, ask — it might be available as a special. The Mangalorean Ghee Roast has technically been a "special" for two years now.

What's the kitchen closing time?

Last food orders are taken at 12:30AM. The bar runs until 2AM on weekdays and later on weekends. The rooftop dining closes at 1AM but transitions to cocktails until 4AM Friday–Saturday. We never rush you through a meal.

Practical Matters

Is parking available?

Limited, and honestly, we recommend against it. Bandra on a Friday night. You know what we mean. Valet is available from 8PM at ₹200. We strongly suggest Uber, Ola, or auto — Bandra station is 7 minutes away, which means so is the rest of the city.

Is the venue accessible for guests with mobility requirements?

Yes. The building has a lift that serves all floors, accessible bathrooms on every floor, and ramp access at the main entrance. Please call us ahead of your visit so we can ensure the right table is prepared for you.

What happens if I lose something?

Call us as soon as you realise. We have a lost & found that we take seriously. Most things — phones, wallets, keys — turn up because our staff are honest people. For items not claimed within 30 days, we donate clothing and dispose of perishables. We'll hold everything else for 60 days.

Do you have gift cards?

We do. Digital gift cards in denominations of ₹1,000, ₹2,500, ₹5,000, and ₹10,000. Redeemable against food, beverages, spa treatments, and pool passes. Not redeemable for memberships (those require an application, not just money). Email us at hello@wellbeing.in to purchase.

Do you have merchandise?

A small selection. A tote bag. A tee. A very good candle that smells like the spa floor. Available at the front desk. We don't have an online store yet — mostly because we haven't decided if we want to be the kind of place that has an online store.

Can I host a proposal here?

Yes. We've done a few. Speak to the reservations team — they handle it with appropriate levels of discretion and excitement. We don't charge extra for this. We do appreciate being told the answer was yes.

Is smoking allowed?

The rooftop has a designated smoking area at the north end. Smoking is not permitted anywhere else in the building including the pool deck and spa. E-cigarettes follow the same policy — the designated area only.

Do you take cards? UPI? Crypto?

All major cards, UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm). No crypto, not because of a philosophical objection, but because the accounting team said no and they handle the accounting. Cash is accepted but the billing system is digital, so change may be slower than usual.

Can I book Wellbeing for a TV show or film shoot?

Sometimes. We've been approached several times and said yes twice. It depends on the project, the team, and how much of our regular operation it disrupts. Email press@wellbeing.in with details. Don't email reservations — they'll just forward it and it'll take longer.

Still Have Questions?

We're actual people.

If you can't find an answer here, email us at hello@wellbeing.in. We typically respond within a business day. If it's urgent, call the front desk — someone is always there from noon until 3AM.
