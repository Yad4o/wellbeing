Bar Director's Note

A drink should
tell you something.

The bar programme at Wellbeing is built on a single principle: every drink should have a reason. A reason for the base spirit, a reason for the acid, a reason for the garnish. If we can't explain why something is in the glass, it's not in the glass.

We work with Indian spirits where they outperform the imported alternative. We work with local citrus — Byadagi lemon, Coorg orange — because the flavour profile is different here, and that difference is worth building around.

The bar closes last. Always.
