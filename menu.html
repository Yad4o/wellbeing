The Table

Indian coastal cuisine, reimagined without apology. Chef Kavya Nair's menu changes with the tide, the season, and her mood.

Back to Home

The Philosophy

Food that earns
your attention.

We don't do fusion for the sake of it. Chef Kavya Nair grew up eating fish curry in Mangalore and trained in Lyon. The menu is the result of those two places refusing to stay separate. Everything here is from somewhere specific. The Madras prawn. The Goan toddy vinegar. The Andhra chilli that makes you sweat and smile at the same time.

All dishes subject to seasonal change. Allergy and dietary information available on request. We cook with butter, cream, and conviction.
